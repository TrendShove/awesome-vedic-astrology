# Vedic Astrology Chart Interpretation Guide 🌌

Welcome to the **Vedic Astrology Resource Repository**. This project is dedicated to demystifying the ancient "Science of Light" (Jyotish) and providing a structured framework for reading and interpreting Vedic birth charts (Kundali).

Whether you are a data scientist interested in astronomical calculations or a spiritual seeker, this guide provides the foundational steps to move from a "diamond shape" diagram to a deep life narrative.

## 📋 Overview of the System

Vedic astrology uses the **Sidereal Zodiac**, which is anchored to the actual, observable positions of the constellations. This repository focuses on the **North Indian Diamond Chart** layout, where the houses are fixed, and the signs rotate based on your Ascendant (Lagna).

### Key Learning Pillars:
- **The 12 Houses (Bhavas):** Understanding the "where" of your life (Career, Wealth, Family).
- **The 9 Planets (Navagrahas):** Identifying the cosmic "actors" delivering karmic results.
- **The 12 Signs (Rashis):** Decoding the "flavor" and style of planetary expression.
- **Dasha Systems:** Mapping the cosmic timeline to predict life events.

## 🚀 Getting Started with Chart Reading

To accurately read a Vedic chart, precision is mandatory. Even a few minutes' difference in birth time can shift your **Lagna (Ascendant)** and alter your entire house placement.

### Practical Interpretation Steps:
1. **Identify your Lagna:** Locate the first house (top-center diamond).
2. **Map the Houses:** Follow the counter-clockwise sequence to identify life domains.
3. **Assess Planetary Dignity:** Determine if planets are exalted, debilitated, or in "friendly" signs.
4. **Analyze Drishti (Aspects):** Understand how planets "glance" at and influence each other across the chart.
